Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

At Galactic Words, we prioritize your privacy above all. This policy outlines how we handle your information to ensure a secure and enjoyable cosmic journey.

1. Information Collection

To provide a personalized experience, we collect minimal data:

  • Account Credentials: When using Apple Sign In, we receive a unique identifier and your basic profile name.
  • Galactic Progress: We store your level data, achievements, and currency balance across the stars.
  • Cosmic Performance: Anonymous data regarding app stability and performance to help us improve the experience.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is strictly used for:

  • Synchronizing your progress across different devices.
  • Providing customer support and technical assistance.
  • Analyzing game patterns to create more challenging and fun levels.

3. Security Standards

Your data is protected using industry-leading encryption. We utilize Firebase Cloud Services to ensure your information is stored in highly secure, world-class data centers.

4. Third-Party Partners

We work with trusted partners to enhance your experience:

  • RevenueCat: Secure management of in-app galactic purchases.
  • Firebase: Real-time database and authentication services.
  • Apple: Secure sign-in and ecosystem integration.

5. Your Rights & Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete your entire account instantly within the app's Settings > Account section, or contact us via email for manual removal.
